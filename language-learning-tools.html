What do I use to learn languages?

I love learning languages since I was a grade school kid. I've developed some routines and found some tools that help me stay on top of things and progress. Here are some things I've been using for some of the languages I've learnt. Some of those tools are good to use when you've already reached some fluency (A2/B1 is enough, typically). I marked them with an asterisk. I've written this in English for simplicity, and some of the materials are also in English. They're segregated by language.

German

  • Nachrichtenleicht* - news in simple German, from German Public Broadcaster. Each news item has a wordlist and an audio recording, and there's a separate wordbook;
  • Wiktionary - an online dictionary. Search the word and choose the language
  • Becoming Fluent in German: 150 Short Stories, Philipp Eich - short stories, each with a dedicated wordbook;
