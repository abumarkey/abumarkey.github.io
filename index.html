Hi! My name’s Marek Fronc.

I’m a mobile photographer, and blogger based in Kraków, Poland.

My chief interest is in the politics, society and pop-culture of the Middle East. I had spent four years in Bahrain, where I went to high school. I now study the region at university, at undergraduate level (expected to finish 2022).

In terms of photography, landscape pictures is where I’m at. Most often of cities, as I’m an urban child, yet I venture into the green as well. I have an experience with point and shoot cameras, but moved to mobile photography by 2013. I find smartphones (iPhone 11 currently) to be the quickest and handiest cameras, while offering a good image quality.

I occasionally write about politics and society here. I also have a blog about the Middle East, which I update in Polish. I also speak Czech, and English, as well as learn Arabic and German.

An anarcho-socialist, and a feminist, I also support equal rights for LGBT+ and all ethnicities. Capitalism exploits us, and the ruling class seeks to divide us to divert our attention from it.
