Hi! My name’s Marek Fronc.





I am a mobile photographer based in Kraków, Poland.

I am interested in history, politics and societies of Middle East and North Africa. Before I began Middle East Studies (undergrad, class of 2022), I spent four years in Bahrain and graduated high school there. I still occasionally visit.

I used to write things online. Right now, I focus on city photography, with a little twist of nature shots. I have been using several smartphones to take photos since 2013. That takes my time on instagram.

I like to think I have a knack for languages - I speak Polish, Czech and English fluently, as well as learn Arabic and German.

I'm also a minimalist and a militant cyclist, having mostly ditched mass transit for a trekking bike. We're cooler than the electric car drivers - especially in winter.

An anarchist, and a feminist, I also support equal rights for LGBT+ and all ethnicities. Capitalism exploits us, and the ruling class seeks to divide us to divert our attention from it.
