Hi! My name’s Marek Fronc.

I’m a mobile photographer, and blogger based in Kraków, Poland.

My interests lay in the Middle East. I’d spent four years in Bahrain, and now study the region at university (undergraduate). My focus is pop-culture, society and politics. I’m also interested in those of Europe. I'm focused on countries of the Gulf, but I spy with my little eye on others, too.

In terms of photography, landscape pictures is where I’m at. Most often of cities, as I’m an urban child, but I venture into the green as well.

I occasionally write about politics and society here. I also have a blog about the Middle East, which I update in Polish. I also speak Czech, and English.

An anarcho-socialist, and a feminist, I also support equal rights for LGBT+ and all ethnicities. Capitalism exploits us, and the ruling class seeks to divide us to divert our attention from it.
